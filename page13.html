TVC Fountain of Life in Jesus Christ Church

TVC (The Valley Cathedral)
In 2007, Pastor Abiertas also known as Pastor Lerio conducted a Bible study in Auntie Malinay’s House for her son who has a problem in mind together with some people of Piloy, Caliking,Atok,Benguet.From that time they continued the bible study. After how many years, Auntie Malinay’s Son was finally healed with God’s Grace. Beside Auntie Malinay’s house, they used “Tolsha” as a shade during their services.
 In 2008, Mr. Jackson Bangsit donated 200 square meter land for the church purpose. But according to the main church -Valley Cathedral, it is not easy to occupy a space that was donated so it is better to pay for it even in a small amount because there is a tendency that the owner or the family members will take it back.
 

After 3 years, Ptr.Lerio assigned leaders to take charge of the services in Piloy because he is the one who is in charge in the mother church in Takarang.

 At present, the church was experiencing problems that they want to address. One problem is the road going to the church because it was destroyed by the typhoon Ondoy or Pepeng in the past years. Current or electricity is also one of the problems that is why if there are special gatherings, they connect their electrical lines to the neighbouring house. Another problem is the absence of musical instruments since they only have an acoustic guitar.
