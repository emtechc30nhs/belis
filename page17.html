Belis Youth Bayanihan System                 Local  Policies

I: Membership:
1. Ti mabalin nga agmembro iti belis youth bayanihan system (BYBS) ket masapol nga residente ti paticnan, belis, proper, piloy, caliking,ken damsite laeng.Amin nga miyembro ket masapol nga takderan ti parents no awan jay miyembro.
(The members of Belis Youtth Bayanihan System(BYBS) should be a residents of Paticnan, Belis Proper, Piloy, Caliking and Damsite. All members should represent by their parent if they are not around.)

2. Ti mabalin nga agmiyembro ket 15 years old nga agpangato nga awan asawa nan ngem masapol nga takderan ken permaan ti naganak dagiti 15 years old ti edad na nga miyembro.
(The members should be at 15 years old in above and must be single. A member at the age of 15 must be in the supervision of their parents.)

3. Masapol nga ti agmiyembro ket maawatan ken agtungpal iti paglintegan ti organisasyon.
(All members should understand and follow the rules and regulation of the organization.)

4. Ti ag-miyembro ket maki attendar iti meeting/seminar nga maipanggep iti actibidad ti organisasyon.
(All members should attend in any meetings or seminars about the activities of the organization.)

5. Kada ag-miyembro ket agbayad iti: a.) Membership Fee -50.00. b.) Individual Minimum Share – 100.00 with a maximum of P 3,000.00. c.) Nu agsubli jay nag ikkat nga miyembro ket agbayad ti 500.00.

(Every Member must pay:
a.) Membership Fee -50.00. b.) Individual Minimum Share – 100.00 with a maximum of P 3,000.00.
 c.) when the members will returned, he/she will pay 500.00.)

6. Ti panang-akseptar ti baro nga miyembro ket kada adda ti meeting.
(The acceptance of new members will be done every conducting of meeting.)

7. No adda ti new members ket masapol ag uray ti 1 year santo agpa meeting.
( A new member is allowed to conduct a meeting after 1 year of membership.)

II: Responsibilidad ti kada membro:
(RESPONSIBILITIES OF A MEMBER)

1. Agtungpal iti regulasyon ken paglentegan ti oraganisasyon.
(Obey and follow the regulations and rules of the organization.)

2. Maki attendar ti meeting /seminar wenno anyaman nga contribution iti organisasyon.No awan jay miyembro no ada meeting mabalin nga attendaran ti parents.
( Attend meetings/seminar and contribute in any contribution of the organization. When the member is not available or absent, their parent can represent them.)

3. Agbayad ti membership fee, individual share ken anyaman a contribution iti organisasyon.
(Must pay the membership fee, individual share or any contribution of the organization.)

4. Masapol nga makicooperate ti maysa nga miyembro ti pagsayaatan ti organisasyon, kas kada kuma iti panagsuportar iti Fund Raising (Raffle Tickets), ken daduma pay, tapnu ada panga-laan tip undo ti organisasyon.
(Every member should cooperate in the betterment of the organization, like for conducting Fund Raising(Raffle Tickets), etc. which this will serves as the fund of the organization.)

III: Regulasyun Ken Paglintegan ti Organisasyon (Rules and Regulation of the Organization)

1. Dagijay gatad nga inted ti maysa nga miyembro iti ka miyembro-an na nga agkasapulan ket masapol nga isu met ti isubli na kenkuana nu isu met ti makasapol.
(The amount that each member shares to its co-member who is in need will be the amount that will return to him/her when he needs it.)

2. No ti maysa nga miyembro ket saan nga mangted iti maminduwa iti individual share na, ket maikkat a dagus iti organisasyon ta kaawatan nga saan nga interesado.
(when the member will not give his/her share within two contributions will be out of the organization, which it shows that he/she is not interested.)

3. Ngem nu ti aysa nga miyembro ket maikkat ket adda pay laeng utang na iti ka-miyembro-an na, karbengan dagiti membro nga ag singir kenkuana ken kasapulan nga bayadan na.
(when a member is not a member anymore, and he/she is still have obligation to pay, the co-members has the right to ask the unpaid balance and must pay for it.)

4. No ti maysa nga membro ket saan nga maki attendar iti General Assembly Meeting malaksid iti emergency Meeting ket mamulta iti 100.00 ket daytoy ti mainayon ti pundo ti organisasyon. Amin nga miyembro masapol agited t contribution na nu general assembly apan man wenu haan.
 
(When a member (single or married) will not attend the general assembly meeting aside from emergency meeting, he/she will be penalized an amount of 100 pesos which will be included in the fund of the organization. Every member (single or married) should contribute an amount of 100 pesos every general assembly. All members must contribute either will attend or not. )

5. No ti maysa miyembro ket agkedked nga agbayad iti ka-miyembro-an na ket, masingir pay kenkuana ti porsyento na nga 5% monthly.
(When a member opt not to pay in their co-member will be fined 5% per month.)

6. Amin nga miyembro nga ada problema na panggep ti organisasyon ket masapol nga e presentar da nu ada maangay nga meeting, wenno ipakaamo na kadageti opisyales.
(All members with a problem in the co-members should present their issue if there will be a meeting or inform the officers.)
7. No adda agpa meeting nga miyembro, ipaka ammo na iti opisyales iti organisasyon iti personal.
(A member must inform the officers of the organization if they are planning to conduct a meeting.)
8. Amin nga maipanggep ti pundo ti organisasyon ket masapol nga anamongan amin dagiti miyembro, saan laeng nga dageti opisayales ti agdesisyon.
( All officers and members must involve about the funding of the organization.)





9. Maangay ti general assembly meeting kada maudi nga dominggo, bulan ti disyembre kada tawen.
(The conduction of general assembly is every last Sunday of December.)

10. Ti panagsukat dagiti opisyales ket kada malpas ti lima nga tawen(5 years.).
(Election of new officers every will be after 5 years.)

11. No ada agpameeting,dapat koyogen na jay asawaen na.
( a member who will conduct a meeting must accompany his/her husband/wife to be and present to the officers and members.)

12. If in case no ada ti matay, depende jay miyembro, (no kayat na sublien jay kwarta na,isuna mismo ti mangsingir)
(In case the death of a member, this will depends on the member if he/she will get back his/her share.)

13. Every general assembly, ma audit dagiti tools ken usar ti organization.
(every general assembly, the properties will be audited)

14. No adda kayat na nga agmiyembro ngem adaan en ti anak, (haan nga nagkasar) maawat ngem kasapulan nga ag – uray ti 1 taon.
(a membership is open to a couple who did not yet undergo with wedding ceremony but he/she needs to wait at least one year before conducting a meeting.)

15. Maangay ti meeting kada 6 months wennu maminduwa t maysa nga tawen. (compulsory)
( meetings will be conducted every 6 months or twice a year.)

16. Hiring ti usar ti organisasyon. Care – off ijay members/officers
(Hiring of properties of the organization will be care – off the assigned members or officers)

17. Absences dagiti miyembro.(absences of the members)
 a. single – penalty – masuspende nu agpameeting ken deduction ti 200.00 pesos depende iti tawen nga haan na nga inatendaran.
(for single – suspension of conducting a meeting and deduction of 200 pesos depends of the year of his/her contribution.)

 b. married – deduction ti medical assistance no agpameeting.
(for married – deduction of medical assistance when he/she will conduct a meeting.)

 c. emergency case – adda ti valid reason na, ken agtext iti officers wennu agaramid t surat. Dagiti opisyales ken advisers ti agdesisyon nu valid t rason na.
(in case of emergency – should have a valid reason and text the officers or make a letter. The officers and advisers will decide of the validity of the reason.)

IV. Ti Kausaran Daytoy nga Organisasyon (The purpose of the organization)

a.) Wedding Purposes:
*Maysa nga bulan ti paway way kadageti miyembro nga mangesagana iti indibidual share da, sakbay ti petsa ti kasar.
(The preparation of the individual share must be one month before the wedding.)

*Kada miyembro kuma ket masapol nga makaited ti share ti maudi nga panagsubmitar tapno mamingpinsan ti resibo, no saan nga makasangbot iti last submission saanen nga ma- akseptar.
(All members must submit their individual share on or before the given deadline and if it is too late, do not give anymore.)

 *Ti pangtedan ti share kadageti nabutusan nga collector, sadanto met isubmit iti treasurer.
( The share must be submitted to the collectors and the collectors will submit to the treasurer.)

* Ti treasurer ken dagiti sumagmamanu nga opisyales ti organisasyon ti mangted iti miyembro nga makasapol.
( The Treasurer and some of the officials will personally give the collected shares to the concerned member.)

b.) Medical Assistance:
* amin nga miyembro nga agkailangan ti medical assistance
(all members are entitled to acquire medical assistance.)
1. AG ANAK – maminsan
2. AGSAKIT - anytime nga kailangan
(1. Give Birth - only once 2. Sickness - anytime if he/she needs)

* Ipakaamo na iti opisyales ti organisasyon ket makaamun ti collector nga agcollect kadagiti miyembro.
(the concerned member must inform the officers for the collectors to start collecting shares.)

* Agpundo dagiti members iti indibidual share tapno nakasagana nu adda agkasapulan.
(The members may give their individual share anytime for easy pick if there will someone needing it.)

 * No ma confine ti maysa nga miyembro bahala ti collector nga apan ag collect iti kada miyembro ti 50.00 Php. minimum share.
(if just in case that the member is confined, the collector has the initiative to collect the shares of the members in the amount of 50 pesos as minimum share.)
