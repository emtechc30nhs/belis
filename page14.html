Who are the developers?

The developers of the website are the grade 12 students of Camp30 National High School namely: Mayrose Dio-alan, Regina Bayating, Mary Joy Pias, Jhan May Pingaping and Lyn Tiwatiw.

Why?

One of the requirements of the subject, Empowerment Technology, requires us to create our own web page. Thus, we chose sitio Belis as our major project site because we want to prioritize our own place for the development  and promotion of our own products that we produce in our community, organizations between womens and youths, and the different institutions. 

Basically, our main objective is to inform other people on the current situation of the place. Through this web page, we are also hoping for new opportunities to come that might help our community develop and progress.
