                                                               The History of the Church

In October 1989, Rev. Byung Yoon Kim came to Belis to conduct a house to house visitation and Bible study together with Pastor Roland Dellias, Irene Calascas and other members of Ambassador Church. On December 1989, they started the service in Bilis Elementary School. Eventually, many youths were encouraged to accept God in their lives, so the water baptism was then held first at Bauang, La Union on May 1990. After the first baptism, 4 young people decided to study in Luzon Bible College at Binalonan, Pangasinan which was sponsored by Rev. Byung Yoon Kim and company. However, only 2 were able to finish their studies namely: Pastor Eric Mabin and Pastora Myla Herrerra.


In the same year, the next baptism was held  in Orongaw brook headed by Rev. Kim, Bowekaw brook  by Pastor Lito Calbayan and Diyang Brook by Pastor Paul Barcena. On February 2009, the fifth baptism was held in Buguias Central and the sixth baptism was in Itogon on February 6, 2010 by Pastor Jason Simeon. Recently, on April 2017, it was held at Bauang, La union by Pastor Joshua Balite.
