Exploring Belis...

Belis is a small sitio of Barangay Caliking, Atok, Benguet. It is located for about 3 kilometers down from the Halsema Highway at kilometer 22, Caliking, Atok, Benguet. 
Belis has many sub-sitio that comprises Paticnan, Penanshay, Belis Proper, Salat, Naguey, Piket, Piloy, and Gobey.
