        According to an interview with Asthrine Pias (Chairman), the women’s organization started in 2011 by the women of Belis, Caliking, Atok, Benguet.


        From the beginning, some of the women joined the Barangay organization. However, when such complicated situation aroused, they decided to organize their own women organization in sitio Belis since the officers are not updating the members regarding the activities done by the organization. So, they collected 20 pesos per head as their membership fee which was used for a small business like selling processed fish “tinapa” and others. Until such time, it grew bigger which was included in their funds. From the funds, a little percentage was given to a member as she borrow an amount. Aside from borrowing, any member has the right to use the fund in terms of medical assistance. Just in case the death of a member, her share must be given to her family as well as the contribution (Php.20) of the other members. After a short period of time, the organization bought mono block chairs which was used for any occasions in the community with a specific amount of hiring.
