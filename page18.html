                                            Belis Youth Bayanihan System

        Belis Youth Bayanihan System (BYBS) is a youth organization in Belis, Caliking, Atok, Benguet. It has a total of 110 members and 5 advisers as of March 2018.

         According to the interview with the members: Jefferson Pablo, Raffy Pias, Serafino Pias Jr. and Monroe Adais (current Chairman), the BYBS started in January 2006 when Kirino Bayating and Cynthia Acosta got married. They started the organization with the purpose of wedding assistance. The organization intended to have only for boys as members. It started with 20 members. From the initial 20 members, they elected officers (Chairman, Vice Chairman, Secretary, Treasure, Auditors as well as the Advisers).
 
        As the days and months passed by, other youth were encouraged to join the organization. Eventually, even female youths were accepted into the organization. The initial purpose of bayanihan for weddings only was extended to providing medical assistance to the whole community.
 
        At present, BYBS has accumulated materials needed for its bayanihan goals. The accumulated materials include kitchen utensils (plastic plates, plastic cups, pots and others);”tolsha”; musical instruments, chairs.  BYBS is not just simply a youth organization providing wedding and medical assistance but it is also a big help to the community in times of need.
